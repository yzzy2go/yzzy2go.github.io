StarterHacks 2020

BetterBuddy was a hackathon project that I built in a team of 4 students over 24 hours. It is aimed towards people who want to attend workshops and other learning events. Using this web app allows them to meet people with the same mindset and interests in their area so they do not have to go by themselves.

Web Development

I was the Frontend developer. My team's designer created a prototype in Figma gave it to me to replicate the design for a webpage. The most challenging part of this for me was working with forms. I had little experience with them and our project required multiple to allow a user to create a new profile and edit their details. I learned about their syntax, implementation, and validation as I was working on this project.

Wireframes

During the first couple hours of the hackathon I also helped determine the flow of our web app. I made sure to consider the usability as well as how many steps it would take to fully create a profile from our home screen. This process proved to be invaluable since we did not have to worry about the small details during development and design.
