Design System

Consistency is key. Over my co-op term I created multiple style guides and library components in Sketch to be used by development teams across the company.

Ensuring that all elementes were updated from previous UI changes was challenging but I was able to bring all libraries and documentation up-to-date.

Prototypes

An important part of my UX role was ensuring that development teams and clients understood the requirements for a project. Most of my prototyping was done using a combination of Sketch, Marvel and Axure to show mandatory changes and how they would fit into the existing project scope.

I was able to present a new feature to a client during a weekly call and recieved positive feedback as well as the approval to start development on my design.

User Research

I aided in conducting UX research over 9 user interviews. We were gathering feedback on a prototype for a future product so that adjustments could be made before requirements were solidified. After each call, I was also in charge of sorting notes into categories so they could be analyzed for pain points and trends across customers.
