Project Scope

This project required a team of students to create an engineering workspace on campus. We chose to focus on the Rocketry team as we found their goals interesting and wanted to see how we could solve some of the problems they had with their current space.

User Interviews

We first started off by contacting the team to see if they were willing to be interviewed. Fortunately, they were and so we came up with questions regarding their routine, storage, and overall use of the space. On the scheduled day we visited their workshop to take pictures and interview team members. From the information gathered, we discussed what would be relevant to focus on for our project.

Iterative Design Process

After the interviews and ideation we created floorplans of the updated space. Following the design thinking process, we went back to the team to gague what they thought of our designs. We recieved new feedback to incorporate into our final prototype and ended up with a physical representation of the space that could theoretically be made for the team.
