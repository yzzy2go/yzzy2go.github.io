Faculty Website

One large project that was assigned to me included the redesign of a faculty resource website. I started off by creating a wireframe in Figma before adding interactions so I was sure what needed to be developed. In less a month, the website was put together in Wordpress to reflect the updated changes.

Online Course Development

The bulk of my work was to create online courses for the College. I worked in Articulate Storyline to populate a module based off information from the course instructors. Every course had a different theme and I ensured that the styles were consistent over the modules that I developed.

Custom Graphics

For the above-mentioned online modules, I worked in Illustrator to design custom graphics. Some slides did not contain a lot of content so the instructor would ask for an image to fill the space. I was given creative liberty to make something that would fit with the course theme and colours. This resulted in lots of practice to develop my vector-making skills.
